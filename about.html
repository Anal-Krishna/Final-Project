About Nike

Nike, Inc. is a globally renowned sportswear and athletic footwear company.

Founded in 1964 as Blue Ribbon Sports and officially becoming Nike in 1971, the company has grown to become one of the most recognized and valuable brands in the world.

Our Founders

Nike was founded by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight. The company initially started as Blue Ribbon Sports (BRS) in January 1964, with Knight importing athletic shoes from Japan and selling them to athletes at track meets. It officially became Nike, Inc. in 1971. Bill Bowerman, a track and field coach, and Phil Knight, a middle-distance runner, combined their expertise to create what would become one of the world's most iconic and successful sportswear brands.
